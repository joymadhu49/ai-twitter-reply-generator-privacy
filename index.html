Privacy Policy for AI Twitter Reply Generator (by JOY)

Last updated: February 2, 2025

AI Twitter Reply Generator (“the Extension”) is designed to help users generate AI-powered reply suggestions for tweets on Twitter (X). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

The Extension reads tweet content currently displayed on the user's screen to generate reply suggestions. The Extension does not collect personal information such as names, emails, passwords, or payment data.

2. How We Use Information

Tweet text is processed solely to generate an AI-powered reply. Your data is not stored, sold, or used for advertising.

3. Third-Party Sharing

Tweet content is sent only to the AI service you configure (Gemini API, OpenRouter API, etc.). No data is sent to the extension developer.

4. Data Security

Your settings and API keys are stored only on your device.

5. Changes to This Policy

Updates will be posted on this page.
